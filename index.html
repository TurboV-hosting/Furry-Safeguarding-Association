Integrity Through Vigilance

We are a dedicated collective focused on identifying and exposing bad actors within the furry community to ensure a safer environment for everyone.

Notice: We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for illegal or predatory behavior.

Our Mission

The Furry Safeguarding Association (FSA) was founded to act as a shield for the community. We believe that transparency is the best deterrent against those who wish to harm or exploit others. Our goal is to provide a central hub for vetted information regarding individuals who pose a threat to the safety and well-being of the fandom.

What We Monitor

Predatory Behavior

Documenting and exposing individuals involved in grooming or predatory actions against minors.

Zoophilia

Maintaining a strict stance against animal abuse and those who promote or engage in "zoo" content.

Hate & Racism

Exposing groups or individuals spreading white supremacy, racism, or organized hate within the fandom.
